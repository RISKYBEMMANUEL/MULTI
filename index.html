About Us

Our Offering

Now our offering has grown and positioned the company as a national leader in Service delivery in information technology infrastructure development, instrumentation & Control, tactical and Surveillance systems for single and multiple integrated building communication systems and networks. Over the years Multimesh have provided services to the Oil and Gas industry, the Military and Law enforcement agencies in additional to general commercial and private establishments.

Our Guiding Principle

As a responsible corporate entity, Multimesh will continue to contribute and support community projects and charitable organization. We will pay our Tithe, our Tax to government and share our profit and gain with those persons and organizations, which are committed to improving the lives of Widows, Orphans and the Elderly in our community.

Our People

Staff strength of 265 employees: We believe that our success and failure will depend on our people how they are selected, placed and maintained. We are therefore committed to ensuring that our management style, human resources programs, rewards system and work environment would be geared at all times towards maximizing the loyalty, commitment, professionalism, teamwork and productivity of our employees.

Our Background

Multimesh Communication Limited is a leading company providing solutions that help businesses and Government agencies of every size, description and location, operate safely and efficiently. Our history of innovation began with the introduction of the MMDS (Microwave Multiple Distribution System. Today Multimesh Group continues to be an innovator in Project Management Consultancy/design and installation of Terrestrial and Wireless Telecommunications Systems such as Voice/data Structured office Cabling, PABX, Local & Wide Area Networking, Workforce Management Solutions, Urban and Rural Community Telephony, V/Sat/Satellite Earth Stations, Land and Mobile Radios, Sound, Fire alarm, Security and Electronic Engineering.
